The Cube at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD)
October 11 to October 29

What is one thing you want to do before you die? We don’t know when our last day on this planet will be — if you were to die tomorrow, could you say that you truly lived the way you wanted?

One Thing is an interactive installation inspired by the artist's fear of death. Hopes, dreams, and goals are displayed on clotheslines in The Cube, encouraging people to share their aspirations with the public and do things they’ve always wanted to do before dying. One Thing is also the first installation in The Cube that incorporates user input. Visitors who are able to visit The Cube can answer the installation’s important question in person through writing, but anyone who cannot visit The Cube in person can submit an answer through the Submit page of the installation’s website. Users from anywhere in the world can send their answers with a click of a button, adding an international aspect to this project. Thus, digital answers are brought together in a physical space, providing inspiration to and from people in countries all around the world.
